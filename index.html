Case Study 01

Brand Evolution and Modernization

Context

Arrow is a 75-year legacy brand with strong recognition but inconsistent execution across 18 locations and no unified voice or visual identity.

Strategy
  • Define a clear brand position, voice, and visual identity
  • Develop a company tagline and icon representing trust, loyalty, and quality
  • Ensure the identity complements the existing logo but can stand alone over time
  • Ground decisions in customer research and insights
My Role
  • Led end-to-end brand initiative across research, strategy, and execution
  • Partnered with an agency to conduct industry research
  • Translated insights into messaging and positioning
  • Presented to executive leadership and drove buy-in
  • Directed creative development of icon and visual system
  • Established brand standards across all channels
Approach
  • Internal: Reinforced adoption through visible brand integration and cultural alignment
  • External: Executed multi-phase rollout including awareness, countdown, and launch storytelling
  • Brand Extension: Developed "Arrow Gives Back" program to connect brand with community engagement

Impact

Transformed a fragmented legacy brand into a unified, scalable identity that strengthened customer trust, improved internal alignment, and created a foundation for consistent growth.
